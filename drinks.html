Our available drinks:

Expresso

Expresso is a full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in "shots."

Americano

An Americano is a popular coffee drink that consists of a single or double shot of espresso diluted with hot water. The result is a beverage that has the strength of a traditional brewed coffee but with the distinct flavor and crema of an espresso.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink traditionally prepared with equal parts double espresso, steamed milk, and steamed milk foam on top. It's distinguished by its frothy foam topping, often sprinkled with cocoa powder or cinnamon. A classic cappuccino is enjoyed for its bold coffee taste and creamy texture.

Latte

A latte, short for "caff√® latte," is a coffee beverage made with espresso and steamed milk.

Mocha

A mocha is a chocolate-flavored variant of a latte. It combines the strong flavors of espresso with the richness of chocolate and the creaminess of steamed milk, typically topped with whipped cream and a dusting of cocoa powder, making it a favorite indulgence for coffee and chocolate lovers alike.

Chocolate River

"Chocolate River" could refer to a rich, chocolate-heavy drink, akin to a hot chocolate with potentially added flavors or a chocolate syrup swirl. If it's a coffee-based beverage, it may be similar to a mocha but with a more pronounced chocolate presence.

Flat White

A flat white is an espresso drink that originated in Australia or New Zealand. It's similar to a latte but with a thinner layer of microfoam, resulting in a velvety consistency.

Raf

Raf coffee is a Russian invention that has gained popularity for its creamy texture and sweet taste. It's made by whipping together cream, vanilla sugar, and espresso until smooth and velvety. The result is a rich, dessert-like coffee that's both comforting and luxurious.
