Our available food:

Eggs with Canadian Sausage

Eggs with Canadian sausage is a hearty and satisfying breakfast dish that combines the rich flavors of eggs and the distinct taste of Canadian sausage, often referred to as Canadian bacon.

Omelette

An omelette is a classic dish made by beating eggs and then cooking them in a frying pan until they're firm, often folded around a filling. The exterior is golden and slightly crispy, while the interior remains soft and fluffy. This versatile dish can be enjoyed plain or with various fillings, making it a breakfast favorite around the world.

Omelette with Vegetables

This variation of the traditional omelette includes a mix of fresh vegetables such as bell peppers, onions, spinach, mushrooms, and tomatoes.

Omelette with Cheese

A cheese omelette is a sumptuous version of the basic omelette, filled or topped with one or more types of cheese, such as cheddar, mozzarella, or feta. As the omelette cooks, the cheese melts, creating a creamy, gooey texture and a rich, savory flavor that perfectly complements the eggs.

Oatmeal

Oatmeal is a warm, comforting cereal made from oats. It is often cooked in milk or water and can be served sweet or savory.

Toaster Bread

Toaster bread refers to slices of bread that are perfect for toasting, turning golden brown and crispy when heated. It can be made from various types of bread, including white, whole wheat, rye, or sourdough.

Club Sandwich

A club sandwich is a type of sandwich traditionally made from toasted bread, sliced cooked poultry, ham or fried bacon, lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise. It's often cut into quarters and held together by cocktail sticks.

Vegan Sandwich

A vegan sandwich is a plant-based version of the traditional sandwich, free from animal products. It often includes ingredients like hummus, avocado, fresh or grilled vegetables, vegan cheeses, and meat substitutes, all sandwiched between slices of vegan bread.
